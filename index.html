Penetration Testing

Penetration testing (pen testing) is a controlled, ethical process that simulates real-world attacks to evaluate the security of systems, networks, and applications. By identifying vulnerabilities before malicious actors do, pen testers help organizations prioritize fixes, strengthen defenses, and reduce risk. The work combines automated scanning with manual analysis and creative problem-solving to probe for misconfigurations, weak authentication, and other security gaps—always performed with permission, a clear scope, and responsible disclosure practices.
